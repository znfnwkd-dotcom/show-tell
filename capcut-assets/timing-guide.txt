===================

1. Import audio/background.mp3 first.
2. Put it at 00:00 on the timeline.

Caption timing:

2. 07.00s - 09.00s | This is my watermelon fan.

4. 15.00s - 18.00s | Stay cool with my fan!
5. 19.00s - 22.00s | Thank you.

Suggested CapCut character motion:
- Put each transparent PNG above its matching background.
- Apply a gentle loop animation: Swing / Float / Breathe if available.
- Or keyframe manually: start scale 100%, middle 104%, end 100%; rotation -2 to +2 degrees.
- Scene 2-4 can use slightly more side-to-side motion to make the fan feel active.
